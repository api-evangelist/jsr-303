We’re thrilled to announce the final release of Jakarta NoSQL 1.0 , a brand-new specification designed to bring standardization, productivity, and portability to NoSQL integration in the Java ecosystem. This release marks a significant step in the Jakarta EE platform by introducing the first-ever NoSQL specification . Whether you’re working with document, key-value, column, or graph databases, Jakarta NoSQL is built to streamline your development experience. Why Jakarta NoSQL?
